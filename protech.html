WE CHANGE THE CHEMISTRY, NOT THE WATER

Protech Group was established in 2006 with the aim of provoding global service for water and wastewater treatment chemicals. The company is established with domestic capital. The company is based in Izmir and has production facilities in Istanbul and in Izmir. In total, it has a production capacity of 30000 tons of liquid 10000 tons of powder products per year.

Protech Group provide products services for;

  • Water Treatment Chemicals
  • Wastewater Treatment Chemicals
  • Mining Chemicals
  • Geothermal Water Treatment Chemicals

The experienced expert staff in the field Protech Group serves both Turkey abroad. Along with its dealers, it continues its customer-oriented working policy with its technical staff of 40 people.

Protech Group has ISO 9001 & 14001 & 18001 & NSF certificates.

Protech Water Technologies is a leading provider of chemical services for water treatment in various industries, including power generation, oil and gas, food and beverage, and healthcare. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing customized solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. We use the latest technology and equipment to ensure that our clients' water systems are running efficiently and effectively while complying with all applicable regulations.

Our chemical services include chemical cleaning, chemical treatment, and chemical supply. We offer a range of chemical cleaning services to remove scale, rust, and other deposits from water systems. Our chemical treatment services are designed to prevent corrosion, scaling, and biofouling. We also provide chemical supply services, including the delivery of chemicals to clients' facilities.

Protech Water Technologies is committed to delivering high-quality chemical services to our clients. We continuously monitor and evaluate our services to ensure that we are providing the best possible solutions for our clients' needs. Contact us today to learn more about our chemical services and how we can help you optimize your water systems.
