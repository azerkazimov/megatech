From Concept to Completion: OZMEN Mechanik Engineering's Comprehensive Industrial Services

OZMEN mechanik engineering is a leading engineering company that specializes in providing mechanical engineering solutions to various industries. With a team of experienced engineers and state-of-the-art technology, OZMEN mechanik engineering offers a wide range of services, including design, manufacturing, installation, and maintenance of machinery and equipment.

OZMEN mechanik engineering is a leading in pipeline systems of oil refineries. Manufacture of various high-quality (alloyed-non-alloyed) piping systems, including stainless or carbon steel. We carry out both the production of coils and the production and assembly on site, and at the last stage we carry out hydrostatic tests and put them into operation.

We provide in-plant piping of these chemical-resistant pipes up to the commissioning stage, after instrumental assembly and air testing.

At OZMEN mechanik engineering, our focus is on delivering high-quality solutions that are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We use the latest technology and industry-standard practices to ensure that our projects are completed on time and within budget.

We also perform the installation of fire piping and hydrants and lines in industrial facilities (refinery, airport tank farms, thermal power plants, etc.). We install systems on the tank, such as sprinkler, in LPG facilities. We perform the installation of systems on the tank such as sprinkler in LPG facilities. We perform foam chamber installation in tank farms, installation of fire lines around the tank in field tanks

Whether you need a complete mechanical engineering solution or support with a specific aspect of your project, OZMEN mechanik engineering is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve your goals.
